When your game crashes and you decide to send us a bug report, here's the information we receive:

The info you care about *more than we do*:

  • Your Steam Persona (Public Steam Name)
  • Your Steam ID
  • Your Operating System

The info we care about *more than you do*:

We send a bunch of in-game variables based on what you were doing when the game crashed. So if you were in a stage, it would send your replay and ghost information along with the crash. BUT, if you were in the editor, it will send us the level you were making that caused the error! It's all situational and changing constantly but it's the most important info of the bunch!
